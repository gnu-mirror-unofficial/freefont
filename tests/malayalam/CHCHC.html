But even here there is an inconsistency. The nn.dd.ya is shown with a ligated nn.dda, which is not in the reformed canon of conjunct ligatures. Likewise with the two conjuncts wiht n.dha.

This document shows rra = U+0D31 behaving identically to ra = U+0D30 in every conjunct, except for the one with itself, rr.rra, where the glyph is stacked.

But I find no fonts show rra with the ra-like behavior. In fact almost all of them show it forming a chillu with na, as well as the stacked form with itself.

The result is that I find no fonts that agree with 4 of the 12 conjuncts mentioned here. And 3 more seem to contradict the government standard.
