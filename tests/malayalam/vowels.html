vowel ligatures in Malayalam

The ZWJ before a vowel (or vocalic) should force a ligature if one exists, and the ZWJ before a vowel should inhibit a ligature.

The display for the ZWJ and ZWNJ forms should not be affected by orthography.

Not every consonant with an -u form has a -uu form.
