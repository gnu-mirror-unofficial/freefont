ZWJ test in Malayalam

According to ch. 12 of the Unicode standard on Malayalam: "The sequence <C1, ZWJ, virama, C2> may be used to request traditional ligatures".

common consonant conjuncts

To form a conjunct of three consonants, a ZWJ should be placed before each of the two intervening candrakalas.

with candrakkala la

The original reformed orthorgraphy order prescribes subscript -la beneath only a few consonants. With ZWJ it can be requested to appear beneath any consonant.


conj.
+oo		 ZWJ
conj.		 ZWJ
+oo
k.la ക്ല ക്ലൌ ക‍്ല ക‍്ലൌ

vowel conjuncts

The ZWJ character can also be used where no virama is present. (This functionality is inferred from other sections of the Unicode standard.) Without ZWJ, the traditional and reformed orthography representations are different. With ZWJ, they should be the same in either orthography.
